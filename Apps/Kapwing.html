How to edit a video online

  1. Upload video footage
    Upload your original video footage to Kapwing's advanced media library. Media library will convert and optimize your uploads and serve as an easy area for organizing all your clips. Once you're ready to assemble your video, drag and drop clips to the timeline or canvas.
  2. Edit video clips
    Using the timeline, trim and split clips until you have highlighted the key parts of your footage. Append these clips together to assemble your full story. Add text, audio, animations, and any other elements that you need to make your video stand out.
  3. Export and share
    Once you're done, hit the green "Export" button and your final video will be generated in just a few seconds. Unlike other video editors, Kapwing works completely online for free. Download your final video and share it on your favorite social media platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to try Kapwing's video editor?

Yes, the Kapwing video editor is available for free—you don't even need an account to start editing. There are some limitations around features and video length for users on the free plan, and a small watermark is applied.

What devices and browsers does Kapwing work with?

Kapwing works on any device and browser, though we recommend Chromium-based browsers like Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. Kapwing also works on iOS and Android from mobile devices straight from your browser. Since Kapwing is web-based software, it works across Windows, Mac, and other desktop devices, too.

What are some of Kapwing's best features?

Kapwing is built around speed and collaboration. For individual creators, features like our magic subtitles, automatic silence removal, one-click video resizer, instant transcript and translations, and the ability to edit video just by editing text are all very popular.

What video files does Kapwing work with?

Kapwing's video editor works will all popular file types for video (MP4, AVI, MOV, etc.). Though note that video exports done in Kapwing will always be MP4, and audio files will always be MP3. We feel these files represent the best tradeoff between file size and quality.

What is the best free video editor?

Kapwing, rated 4.9 on Google reviews, is the internet's top free online video editor. It balances powerful video editing capabilities with user-friendly design. Start with a template or your footage, using the timeline for various edits. Kapwing's free version is ad-free and doesn't require a download, making it the ideal editor for fast-moving creatives and teams.

Why should I edit videos online?

Online video editors outshine traditional software by offering a faster, more accessible editing experience. These cloud-based platforms facilitate collaboration, centralize storage, support powerful extensions, and provide a wealth of tutorials and templates. With no downloads or installations, anyone can get started with video editing.

How do you make video edits?

Video editing is about crafting a story through clips. Kapwing's timeline tool simplifies this process with non-linear editing, trimming, and previewing. Users can visually add text, music, and effects to any-length videos. A clear vision, coupled with an easy-to-use tool like Kapwing, results in compelling edits.
